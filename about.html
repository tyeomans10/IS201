About Me

This is who I am

I am a junior at Brigham Young University studying finance and preparing for a career in asset management. I am especially interested in public equities, fixed income, private credit, and the broader alternatives space. I enjoy developing my analytical and investment skills through hands-on experience, including work in student investment funds and ongoing preparation for the CFA program.

Much of my experience has centered on research, valuation, and portfolio decision-making. Through roles in organizations such as the BYU Silver Fund and Brigham Capital, I have built financial models, presented investment ideas, led teams, and contributed to real portfolio outcomes. I am motivated by opportunities that require critical thinking, thoughtful analysis, and a strong understanding of markets.

Outside of academics and investing, I stay active and grounded through outdoor and athletic hobbies. I enjoy rock climbing across multiple states, skiing in the winter, and playing basketball whenever I can. I also spend time fishing and exploring new places. These activities help me stay balanced and continually push myself to grow both personally and professionally.
