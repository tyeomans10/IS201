AWARDS, SKILLS & INTERESTS

Striving to Achieve Balance and Growth in All Areas of Life

I graduated as the valedictorian of Corner Canyon High School, finishing first in a class of 585 students. I also earned the rank of Eagle Scout in 2020, completing a service project where I raised funds and planted noninvasive trees to support a local wetland restoration effort.

Outside of academics and service, I enjoy staying active and spending time outdoors. I am an experienced fisherman, having caught more than 20 species across six states in both freshwater and saltwater. I also enjoy rock climbing in multiple formats, including sport climbing, bouldering, and traditional climbing.
