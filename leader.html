LEADERSHIP & SERVICE

Committed to Lifelong Service

From October 2023 to October 2024, I volunteered at Centro Hispano in Provo, UT as part of the Low-Income Taxpayer Clinic. I helped organize and promote educational classes on topics such as taxes and marketing for members of the Hispanic community. I also worked closely with Hispanic business owners, teaching classes designed to help small business owners better understand and manage their finances.

From July 2021 to July 2023, I served as a Full-Time Volunteer Representative for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Lansing, MI. During this time, I performed weekly service in communities across Michigan, partnering with reputable nonprofit organizations. For 16 months of my service, I held leadership roles where I trained and supported groups of up to 180 representatives, helping them strengthen essential skills and improve their service efforts.
