EDUCATION

Brigham Young University

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Finance at the Marriott School of Business at Brigham Young University and am expected to graduate in December 2026. I have maintained a 4.00 GPA and earned a 34 ACT score, placing me in the 99th percentile. I was awarded a full academic scholarship and have passed the CFA Level I Exam, strengthening my technical understanding of investment principles and financial analysis.

In addition to academics, I serve as the Vice President of the Private Equity, Private Credit, and Venture Capital Association, where I help support student engagement in the alternative investments space. I also represented BYU as the Team Captain for the 2024 Point72 National Stock Pitch Case Competition, leading the team in presenting a short thesis on Cava (NYSE: CAVA). These leadership and experiential opportunities have helped me refine my analytical skills, collaboration abilities, and long-term interest in a career within asset management and private markets.
